Halloumi Burger Supreme

Check this out.. Halloumi, Aubergine, Courgette and Pepper 'burger' with home made sweet chilli ketchup and dairy free mayonnaise.This will make all the meat eating BBQ heads envy you this summer!!

Ingredients

  • 1 Courgette
  • 1 Aubergine
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 5 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 1 x 200g pack Halloumi cheese
  • 4 Burger buns or wholemeal pitta
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • 50g Rocket or similar leaves

Preparation

  1. Cut the courgette in half and then into strips lengthwise, slice the aubergines lengthwise into thin strips and chop the pepper vertically into four large sections
  2. Chop the halloumi into 4 thin rectangular slices.
  3. Put the cut veg into a large bowl with 4 tablespoons of the oil and the salt and mix well until they all have a nice even coating.
  4. Start to grill the prepared veg on a hot barbecue, turning regularly â€“ you want them nicely charred but also soft and tender.
  5. Remove your cooked veg from the grill and set aside while you cook the halloumi â€“ it will cook very quickly! Put it on the barbecue until it has nice grill marks on each side, about 5 minutes.
  6. Toast the buns on the barbecue, then layer them up with some ketchup on the bottom bun, mayo on the top bun, grilled courgettes, aubergine, halloumi, roasted peppers and some rocket in between.
  7. Take the first bite... epic, and oh so tasty!
